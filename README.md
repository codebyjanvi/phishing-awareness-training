⚠️ What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, and bank details.

🔍 How to Recognize Phishing
📧 Phishing Emails
Suspicious sender
Urgent messages
Grammar mistakes
Unknown links
🌐 Fake Websites
Lookalike URLs
Missing HTTPS
Fake login pages
🧠 Social Engineering

Attackers use:

Urgency
Fear
Trust

This technique is known as Social Engineering.

🛡️ Prevention Tips
Do not click unknown links
Enable 2-Factor Authentication
Verify before sharing data
Use strong passwords
